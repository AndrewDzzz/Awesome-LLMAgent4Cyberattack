Defensive playbook

Controls that stay effective even as lures and automation get smarter.

Identity & access

  • Phishing-resistant MFA where possible (passkeys / FIDO2 keys).
  • Least privilege, time-bound access, and strong recovery policies.
  • Privileged actions require step-up auth and approvals.

Workflow verification

  • Payment changes, vendor onboarding, and HR/IT requests: out-of-band verification.
  • Make ‚Äúslow down & verify‚Äù culturally normal.
  • Use ticketing + audit trails for sensitive changes.

Detection & response

  • Centralized logs, alerting, and anomaly detection on accounts and endpoints.
  • Tabletop exercises: practice containment and recovery.
  • Backups tested via restore drills, not just ‚Äúwe have backups‚Äù.

Training that actually works

  • Train behaviors, not ‚Äúspot the typo‚Äù.
  • Teach red flags: urgency, secrecy, unusual channels, new payment routes.
  • Reward reporting and near-miss sharing.
